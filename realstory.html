Real Story

J. Paperson, originally known as James McPaperson, was a real piece of paper. He was born in a 6th Grade classroom out of a notecard, I think. The moment I made him, I instantly became attached. It's absurd to think but I genuinely loved this small, triangular piece of paper. As you can probably guess, he died within minutes.

One of my other classmates was weird. Simply, weird. I was friends with one of his friends, and so we were friends. When I showed McPaperson to him, he decided to take it out of my hands and eat it. Thus, concluded the life of James McPaperson.

I irrationally started crying over a piece of paper, and it affected me enough that I dedicated every future thing I did to him. It's a miracle I have the photo I do of him, because it's literally the only one. I've since recovered emotionally, obviously, but that small triangle smiley guy had a large impact on my mindset at the time.

In the event I create a company, its name will likely be along the lines of McPaperson or Paperson.
